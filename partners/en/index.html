Business and local partners

OurPins is looking for early partners willing to test listings with stores, venues, local organizations, and event teams.

Sponsored Pin Area features Group coupons Listing reports

01What OurPins is

OurPins helps friends, couples, and families save places they want to visit, then turn those places into plans and memories. We do not want to become an ad-first app. We want partner placements to reach people at the moment a place is becoming something they may actually visit together.

Partner placements are always labeled with “Ad,” “PR,” or “Sponsored.” They are never disguised as posts from friends.

02Possible placements

Sponsored Pin

A clearly labeled recommendation inside maps or spot discovery surfaces.

Area feature

Station areas, campus areas, weekend routes, date plans, and local guides.

Group coupon

Offers designed for friends who are deciding where to go together.

Partner page

Photos, short copy, links, and simple reporting for saves and views.

03Good fits

  • Cafes, restaurants, bars, dessert shops, and local retail
  • Date spots, activities, tourism venues, and event organizers
  • Shopping streets, tourism associations, municipalities, and campus-area projects
  • Hotels, hostels, and local maps for travelers

04Early partner test

We are currently testing the right format before publishing a large rate card. Early partners can help us validate placement design, offer types, and reporting.

All listings are reviewed for user safety, fit, and clear ad labeling.

FEATURES

Four screens that turn "want to go" into real plans.

Discover, decide, go, and look back. OurPins gently connects plans with friends through the bottom tabs.

01 Map

See where everyone wants to go

The places your friends want to go gather on one map. Pin cafés, food, and outings by category, with notes and photos attached. See who wants to go where at a glance.

  • Pin by category (café / Japanese / shopping …)
  • Add notes and photos to each pin
  • The whole group's "want to go" on one map
02 Spots

Find popular spots in a tap

The places gathering the most reactions, shown as a ranking. Filter by Hot / New / Nearby / Been there to see what your group is into right now.

  • Ranks the places that gather reactions
  • Filter by Hot, New, Nearby, Been there
  • Discover spots popular among your friends
03 Plan

Turn candidates into a plan

Suggest candidate dates and vote. Based on everyone's free time, OurPins proposes a day that works. Send the decided plan straight to your device calendar.

  • Suggest candidate dates and decide by vote
  • Proposes a workable day from free time
  • Syncs with your device calendar
04 Memories

Places you visited become memories

Turn the places you visited into memories with a photo and a few words. Your history naturally builds up as your group's album.

  • Record with a photo + a few words
  • Automatically gathered into the group album
  • Look back on the places you've been
HOW IT WORKS

Three steps to make you want to go out.

No complicated setup. Create a group, drop pins, and head out.

1

Create a group

Invite friends or your partner with just an invite code. Share one map together.

2

Pin the places you want to go

Pin the cafés and shops you're curious about. React casually with "want to go / really / been there".

3

Make a plan, keep the memory

Decide a plan from the candidates, then turn the outing into a photo memory. Additions and reactions sync to everyone in real time.

MORE

Made to feel good between friends.

Small touches that make sharing your everyday "kind of want to go" effortless.

Reactions

Share how you feel with "want to go / really / been there". You can see who tapped, too.

Invite codes

Join a group just by sending a code. Share one map right away.

Real-time sync

Additions and reactions show up on everyone's screen instantly. Share one map even when you're apart.

Notifications

Get a push when there's a new pin or a plan is decided. Never miss it.

Shared only within the group

Sharing stays among members. Places and memories are never made public.

Notes & photos

Attach notes and photos to a pin to keep the "why I want to go", too.
